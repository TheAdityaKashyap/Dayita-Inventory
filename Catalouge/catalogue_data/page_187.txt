Characteristics  Applications 
DH 309-15 is a basic coated electrode yielding 25%Cr-12Ni weld metal. Electrode is
producing high strength stainless steel weld metal having good oxidation resistance up to
1100C. The weld metal possesses excellent resistance to corrosion and oxidation Ideal for
welding 18/8 type steels to mild steels clad aide of 18/8 clad steels lining of 12% Cr steels
on mild steels overlays of ferritic steels dissimilar steels and difficult to weld steels.

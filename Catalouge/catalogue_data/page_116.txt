Characteristics  Applications 
Nimotherme-NM1 is a low-hydrogen electrode which contains about 1% Nickel and 0.5%
Molybdenum. This electrode can be welded without PWHT. Some typical applications
include the welding of similar composition high-strength low-alloy or microalloyed
structural steels.

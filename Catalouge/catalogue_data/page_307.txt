Characteristics  Applications 
Autotherme-1 is a copper coated mild steel GMAW wire for welding of mild steel low
carbon steel and other structural steels of tensile strength up to 540 MPa The wire burns
with smooth arc and minimum spatter under optimum welding conditions. It is designed
for single and multi-pasa welding of low and medium carbon steels like SA-36 A/B/C/D
grades of SA-283 A/B/C grades of SA-285 A/B grades of SA-414 etc.

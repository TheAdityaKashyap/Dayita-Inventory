tensile (containing Nickel Molybdenum) steels structures subjected to dynamic loading.

deposits. Presence of Nickel  Molybdenum in the weld metal imparts an excellent crack
resistance  toughness properties. Maxfil-34B (MDH) is suitable for single and multi-pass
welding of similar composition steels fine grained steels and equivalent grade steels like

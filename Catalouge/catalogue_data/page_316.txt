Characteristics  Applications 
Autotherme Cr-Mo 6 is a copper-coated solid wire for GMAW available in bright finish.
The wire gives stable arc smooth welding performances and deposits radiographic quality
welds It is suitable for welding 5%Cr 0.5Mo steel. The weld metal possesses good high
temperature properties. The weld metal possesses excellent creep property up to 550C.
Especially suitable for welding of pipes and tubes of matching composition in Power
plants Refineries Petrochemicals Fertilizer plants ete. Suitable for welding of ASTM
steels Grade FS Pia  F21 of SA-182 Grade T4 T22 of SA-199 Grade T22 of SA-214
Grade C5 of SA-217 Grade P22 of SA-435 Grade FS  F21 (clase 13) of SA-336 Grade

Characteristics  Applications 
DH 4150 is a specially designed electrode to match the heat treating properties of SAE
4130 and 8650 materials. Basic coated low hydrogen all position electrodes. The electrode
shows very good features quite and stable arc less spatter loss and good bead appearance
It is ideally suited for joining and surfacing of SAK 4130 and 8630 materials. The weld
metal fulfills the NACE MR 01-75 requirements for use on oil field equipment in sour
(H28) gas and oil environment.

Characteristics  Applications 
F 708-6 1s 2 copper coated mild steel wire for GTAW. Suitable for welding of mild steel low
carbon steel and other structural steels of tensile strength up to 540 MPa The wire burns
with smooth arc. It is designed for welding of low and medium carbon steels like SA-56
A/BIC/D grades of 84-283 A/B/C grades of SA-285 A/B grades of SA-414 etc.

Characteristics  Applications 
Maxfil-30H is a low alloy steel flux cored wire designed for surfacing applications to
achieve all-weld metal hardness between 280-440 BHN. The wire gives good performance
under CO shielding with radiographic quality weld deposits Weld metal is free from
cracks possesses good metal-to-metal abrasion resistance and can withstand impact
loading. The wire produces thin  friable slag covering with shiny bead appearance.
Maxfil-30H is suitable for single and multi-pass weld-surfacing of rallers pulleys track
links track rollers crane wheels gear shatts idlers brake shoes conveyor parts etc

Characteristics  Applications 
Maxfil-70A1 is a low alloy steel flux cored wire designed for welding of 0.5%Molybdenum
and 1%sChromium and 0.5%Molybdenum steels. The wire gives optimum performance
under CO shielding with radiographic quality weld deposits. The wire produces thin 
friable slag covering with shiny bead appearance. Maxfil-70A1 is designed for single and
multi-pass welding of similar composition steels and equivalent grade steels like Gr. Fl of
SA-182 and SA-346 Gr. A of SA-204 Gr. T1/Tall1b of SA-209 Gr. WC1 of SA-217 Gr A
of SA-302 Gr. Pl of SA-345 Class 1 of A grade of 54-434 etc.
